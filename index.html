Bio

BTS, also known as Bangtan Boys, is a seven member South Korean boy band that began formation in 2010 and debuted in 2013 under Big Hit Entertainment. The septet are composed of:

  • RM
  • Jin
  • Suga
  • J Hope
  • Jimin
  • V
  • JungKook

BTS is the best-selling artist in South Korean History and holds the best-selling album in South Korea with 'Map of the Soul:7'

Discography

Wings 2016
Love Yourself: Tear 2018

BE 2020

BTS released their first English-language single, "Dynamite", on August 21. It's music video broke the YouTube record for the most viewed premiere, with more than three million viewers, and set a new record for the most viewed video in the first 24hours of release. 'Dynamite' debuted at number one on the US Billboard Hot 100 chart with over 260,000 pure sales- earning BTS their first chart topper and making them the first all-South Koread act to earn a number one single in the United States
